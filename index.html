Shengquan Chen (陈盛泉)

"The clearest footprints are printed on the most muddy road."

I was born in November 1995 in Quanzhou, China. I received the B.E. degree from Xiamen University and the Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University.
I am currently an associate professor in School of Mathematical Sciences, Nankai University, Tianjin, China.

I am interested in understanding the intersection of genetics, genomics and human disease. By developing advanced computational methods, we are better able to investigate the organization and function of the human genome.

I am now looking for motivated graduate/undergraduate students. If you are interested in and want to collaborate with me, feel free to contact me.
